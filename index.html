How Bad Is a BMI of 30?
What Is the Purpose of BMI?

When It Concerns Weight, What Is a BMI of 25.21?

by Melodie Anne

The BMI -- or body mass index -- is a basic calculation of your height and weight. Your physician probably keeps tabs on your rating to determine your risk of developing obesity-related diseases. If your BMI starts lingering towards the high end of the index, like around 25.21, it could be a sign that you are overweight and need to shed some pounds.

Doing the Calculation

You only need two numbers to calculate your BMI: Your most current weight in pounds and your height in inches. Divide your weight by the square of your height, then multiply the answer by 703 to get your body mass index. As an example, if you are 155 pounds and stand 67 inches tall, youll need to divide 155 by 4,489 -- or 67 squared. Multiply your result of 0.034 by 703 to get your index score. In this case, your BMI would be 24.27.

What the Results Mean

Your doctor will want to see a BMI of 18.5 to 24.9, meaning youre at an appropriate weight for your height, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention reports. So if your body mass index is at 25.21, its above the normal range, putting you into the overweight category. If it continues to go up to where it reaches 30.0 or more, your physician will classify you as obese.

Why It Matters

Having a higher than normal BMI is often significant for risk factors associated with chronic health issues. If you have a high rating, it means that your blood pressure is likely to be high and your cholesterol could go up, thus increasing your chances of developing cardiovascular disease later on in life. Plus, your odds of having Type 2 diabetes and certain cancers also increase when your BMI is in the overweight or obese range.

Special Considerations

The body mass index shouldnt be the only thing you look at with regards to your health. Sometimes a high BMI, like one of 25.21, isnt a bad thing. If you have a lot of muscle mass, for example, your index rating could be elevated, since muscle is denser and heavier than fat, although youre not necessarily overweight. The calculation doesnt factor in any waist measurements either. Even though your BMI is borderline high at 25.21, your risk of developing chronic diseases could be as great as someone with an obese score, if your fat is centralized around your midsection. Because other factors come into play with the BMI, your physician will want to do a full physical and check some lab values to get a better picture of your overall health status.
